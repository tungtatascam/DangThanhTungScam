# DangThanhTungScam
Cybercrime tied to “SamFW/SamFW Tool,” linked to Hà Nội developer Đặng Thanh Tùng (Tungtata/tungtata). On June 23, 2026 a family member installed samfw.com software (samfwtoolsetup_v5.4.zip). ~10,000 XMR (~$3M) were withdrawn from a Feather Wallet, data deleted, and the tool replaced with SamFwToolSetup_v5.5.1.zip to reduce evidence.
